About AST

About AcademeForge Scholars Test

The AcademeForge Scholars Test (AST) is a premier national-level scholarship and talent identification examination designed for students studying in Classes 1 through 10. Founded with the vision of discovering hidden academic talent across every corner of India, AST provides equal opportunity to every student — regardless of their school board, region, or socioeconomic background.

Our Mission

To identify, nurture, and reward academic talent across India through a fair, transparent, and comprehensive scholarship examination that empowers students to achieve their fullest potential.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted and impactful scholarship platform, where every deserving student gets recognized and every bright mind finds its path to excellence.

Our Objectives

  • Identify top academic talent at grass-root levels
  • Provide financial support through scholarships
  • Build confidence and competitive readiness
  • Create a national network of scholars

Exam Details

Exam Information

Everything you need to know about the AST examination.

Eligibility

All students currently enrolled in Classes 1 to 10 in any recognized school in India.

Subjects

Mathematics, Science, English, Hindi, Drawing (student can choose applicable subjects).

Exam Mode

Online (mandatory for individual students). Institutions may offer offline/hybrid modes.

Language

Available in English and Hindi. Students may choose their preferred language.

Drawing: Basic shapes, Colouring, Pattern drawing

Science: Plants, Animals, Human body, Environment, Weather
English: Grammar, Comprehension, Essay, Letter writing

Science: Physics, Chemistry, Biology fundamentals
English: Literature, Advanced Grammar, Writing skills
Hindi: Sahitya, Vyakaran, Patra lekhan

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Any student currently enrolled in Classes 1 to 10 in any recognized school in India is eligible to participate in AST.

Visit our website and click "Register as Student." Fill the online registration form with your personal and academic details. You will receive a confirmation email with your registration number.

Yes, registration for AST is completely free for individual students. There are no hidden charges.

You need a valid email ID, mobile number, school details, and optionally your Aadhaar number. A passport-size photo is also recommended.

Minor edits (like phone number or email) can be made by contacting our helpdesk within 48 hours of registration. Name and class changes are not permitted post-submission.

AST covers Mathematics, Science, English, Hindi, and Drawing. Students can choose the subjects they wish to appear for.

Individual student registrations are for online mode only. Schools and institutions can opt for offline or hybrid mode for their students.

Each subject has its own duration: Mathematics (60 mins), Science (60 mins), English (45 mins), Hindi (45 mins), Drawing (30 mins).

No, there is no negative marking in AST. Students are encouraged to attempt all questions.

Students can use a laptop, desktop, or tablet with a stable internet connection and a working webcam for proctoring.

AST offers scholarships at National (up to ₹50,000), State (up to ₹15,000), District (up to ₹5,000), and School level. All top performers receive medals, trophies, and certificates.

Scholarships are disbursed directly to the student's bank account (via parent's account for Class 1–5) within 30 days of result declaration.

Yes! Every student who appears in the AST exam will receive a digitally signed Participation Certificate, downloadable from the student portal.

Results will be declared approximately 30 days after the exam date. Students will be notified via email and SMS.

Visit the Results section on our website and enter your Registration Number, Roll Number, or Name to view your result card.

Fill the Institution Registration form on our website. Our team will review and contact you within 48 hours to complete the onboarding process.

Partner institutions receive: bulk registration support, offline exam facilitation, institution performance report, branding support, and awards for top-performing schools.
